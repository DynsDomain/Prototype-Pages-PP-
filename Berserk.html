Berserk

The World of Berserk is a realitic fictional Seinen Manga or Anime,whichever source of media you enjoy. The series has its interesting viewpoints on humanity, morality, madness, justice, vengence and much more. I fell into the series shortly after the manga of Naruto, a worldwide shounen favorite. The series has ended in the manga format but was far from finished anime wise. I was lost and as I had grown older I realised naruto was too unrealistic in terms of morals and value. That was when I stumbled into the world of Berserk and grew to love the journey through the main protagonist, Guts.Yes, that`s literally his name.
